This site is built for LIS 500 and connects class ideas like intersectionality, implicit bias, and “neutral” technology to real-world examples.

What this site is about

The big takeaway from the first modules is that technology isn’t automatically objective. The way we design, label, rank, and automate things can reinforce existing inequalities — even when no one “intends” harm.

I’m using this site as a place to collect course ideas, share resources, and highlight people pushing for equity in tech. The goal is to keep it readable for someone new to these topics (like a first-year student).
