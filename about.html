Quick intro

I’m Fairoz Khan, a student at UW–Madison. I’m interested in how technology influences people’s opportunities and how “normal” product decisions can create unequal outcomes.

In this class, I’m especially focused on how bias can show up in systems that feel objective — like ranking, recommendations, hiring tools, and automated decision-making.

What I’m trying to learn

  • How intersectionality changes how we evaluate “fairness” in tech.
  • How implicit bias can shape decisions even when people have good intentions.
  • How to communicate these ideas clearly to people encountering them for the first time.
